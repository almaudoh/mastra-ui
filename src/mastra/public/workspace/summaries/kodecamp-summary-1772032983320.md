# KodeCamp Summary

> Source: https://kode.camp

- KodeCamp is an online bootcamp that trains learners for tech careers, offering mentorship, community, and industry‑recognized certifications.
- Over 13k registrations, 775+ finalists, and 750+ training videos across 5 cohorts, reaching 8+ countries.
- Courses are diverse, interactive, and self‑paced, with hands‑on projects and progress tracking.
- Alumni testimonials highlight career transformations, from junior roles to product design and frontend development.
- Backed by industry sponsors; includes career guidance sessions and a talent pool for hiring.
- Emphasis on building a skill economy, empowering a future‑ready workforce through practical, outcome‑focused training.
